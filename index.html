Selling your vehicle has never been easier thanks to our streamlined approach that takes you from the point of first contact with our company, to a free quote in 15 minutes or less. In order to receive a free quote simply complete the Free Quote Form on our home page. Your form will be processed immediately, and your free quote sent directly to the email address that you provided on the free quote form. We are also happy to provide your free quote via telephone.Cash offers are only provided upon the completion and submission of the Cash Offer Form. It is generally a good idea to complete the free quote form first to see if our quote is acceptable to you. While cash offers are firmer than free quotes, they are not guaranteed until the pre-sale inspection of your vehicle is completed. Vehicle inspections are performed at our facility. You may drive your vehicle directly to us, or request that your vehicle be picked up. There is no charge for vehicle pick-up. The pre-sale inspection is a short 10-15 minute process, where we are confirming general details such as year, make, model, mileage, and condition of your vehicle. Upon completion of your pre-sale inspection you will receive a Guaranteed Cash Offer. Guaranteed cash offers are valid for 100 miles or three days, whichever comes first. You may choose a payment method of your choice. Available payment methods are corporate check, bank check, bank to bank wire transfer, or cash.
